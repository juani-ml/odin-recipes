Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. While noodles are cooking, brown ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat until beef is no longer pink.
  4. Drain any excess grease from the skillet, and then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, and salt.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together shredded mozzarella cheese and ricotta cheese.
  7. Spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles.
  9. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture on top of the noodles.
  10. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of the meat sauce.
  11. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  12. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Let lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
  15. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
