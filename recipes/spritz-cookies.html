Spritz Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Food coloring (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together with an electric mixer until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the egg, vanilla extract, and salt to the bowl, and beat until well combined.
  4. Sift the flour into the bowl, and mix until just combined. Do not overmix the dough.
  5. If you want to color the cookies, divide the dough into separate bowls and mix in food coloring as desired.
  6. Fill a cookie press with the dough and press cookies onto the prepared baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  9. Repeat with any remaining dough, and enjoy your delicious spritz cookies!
