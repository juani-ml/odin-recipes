Nordic Granola Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup raw sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup raw pumpkin seeds
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dried cranberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together oats, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds, almonds, and walnuts.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together melted coconut oil, maple syrup, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients over the dry ingredients and stir until evenly coated.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the granola is lightly golden and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool completely.
  8. Once cooled, stir in dried cranberries.
  9. Store the granola in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 2 weeks.
  10. Serve with yogurt, milk, or eat as a snack on its own. Enjoy your delicious Nordic granola!
